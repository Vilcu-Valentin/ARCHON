ANNEX - Observational Data

“The tape started with a conversation between two individuals, discussing the 2021 EuroSpace mission to colonize Mars. They were talking about the success of the mission as if it had already happened. There was no hesitation, no speculation—just facts. I looked into it afterward—there was no such thing as EuroSpace. And in our reality, no manned Mars mission had even been launched at that point. It’s as if they were reporting from a completely different timeline.”
