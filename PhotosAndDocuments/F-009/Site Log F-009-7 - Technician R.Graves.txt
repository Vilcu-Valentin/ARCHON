ANNEX - Observational Data

“We deployed the isotope-based detectors across the entire district, hoping to pick up something, anything. At first, nothing registered, but then the isotopes started decaying faster than they should. It was subtle, but it was there. We tracked the decay rate back to the epicenter—an old industrial plant. The walls were already crumbling, steel beams rusted through as if they'd been sitting in salt water for decades. We had no choice but to evacuate.”
