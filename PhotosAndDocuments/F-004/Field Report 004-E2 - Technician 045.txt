“We were setting up monitoring equipment near one of the fault lines when the air around us got... heavy. It felt like someone was pressing down on my chest. Then, just as suddenly, I was floating—everything in the area was. Our tools, our vehicles, even some loose rocks. We barely had time to react before gravity snapped back, and everything hit the ground hard. My team was lucky, but the truck we were using flipped right over.”
